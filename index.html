Projects

Discount calculator for Eliminated Products for store space planning

The ultimate goal is to identify the products going to be eliminated from the stores and apply suitable discounts based on the past sales data, End of the day stock data, product category, Ranging of the products to the stores, Discount percentage rules. By checking the product's sales pattern over a period of time and predicting whether the product would sell within the elimination planned, if the product stock would clear then no discounts shall be applied and if the product stock will have potential leftovers then based on the stock numbers, discounts shall be applied. For the project, we integrate various source systems from Azure cloud and process the data using Databricks. The Elimination prediction module has been scheduled in the databricks cluster and the resultant files would be saved to the Azure Blob storage for data interpretation.
